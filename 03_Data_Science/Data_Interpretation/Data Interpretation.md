**Data Interpretation** is the step where you **translate** analytical results into **meaningful insight** to make decisions.

It's understanding, validating, and explaining the outputs of models and analyses  in a way that leads to actionable conclusions.

### Explain Results
You need to interpret and understand what do the model results actually mean.

### Evaluate Reliability
Take into account the accuracy, bias, overfitting and precision of the model you're using.

### Extract Insights 
Look for patterns, relationships, anomalies, and make reports.

### Connect to Context
Relate to real world context.
