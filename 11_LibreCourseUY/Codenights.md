#### What's a Codenight?

**Codenights** are a weekly instance where LibreCourseUY members gather in [The Discord Server](https://discord.gg/ENTX7nQ9Cs) to work in projects, ask for code reviews or just discuss. It's more of a discussion instance than a proper class, even though we sometimes dive into specific topics.

  

Even though Codenights started earlier, the first registered Codenight will be 09/05/2026's Codenight, as this is the only I (The Editor) consider good enough to write about, as it was the first where real work was done. All following Codenights **should** be properly registered.

  

Feel free to join us any Saturday, starting at 1800, on our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/ENTX7nQ9Cs).
