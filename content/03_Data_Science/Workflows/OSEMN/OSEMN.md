# OSEMN

OSEMN is a data science workflow with five stages:

- **Obtain**: Collect raw data from files, APIs, databases, logs, or external services.
- **Scrub**: Clean, normalize, and validate data so it is consistent and usable.
- **Explore**: Analyze distributions, outliers, correlations, and patterns to understand the data.
- **Model**: Train and tune statistical or machine learning models.
- **iNterpret**: Explain results, validate business impact, and communicate decisions.

## Typical Output of Each Step

- Obtain -> raw datasets and source metadata.
- Scrub -> cleaned dataset and data quality checks.
- Explore -> charts, summary statistics, and hypotheses.
- Model -> trained models and evaluation metrics.
- iNterpret -> recommendations, reports, and next actions.
